Welcome to Rapids Transit!!

We are a river guide service and canoe rental located on the banks of the Buffalo River in Gilbert, Arkansas. We also offer kayak and raft rentals. Our experienced guides will be happy to suggest river trips tailored to any level--the beginner up to the expert. They will also be happy to accompany you all the way! Stop by our country store and pick up your picnic supplies on the way out. We'll even pack it up for you in waterproof containers. (Just a precaution!) Give us a call to make your reservations.

Our rates are as follows:

  • The Lodge: $85.00
  • Jenny's two-bedroom cabins: $140.00
  • Jenny's three-bedroom cabins: $175.00
  • John's Camp: $40.00

Check-in is at 2:00 p.m. and Check-out is at 11:00 a.m. E-mail Mike for reservations or for more information.
