About DEWA

Dubai Electricity and Water Authority (DEWA) is a publicly owned utility company that provides electricity and water services to the residents of Dubai, United Arab Emirates.

Committed to excellence, sustainability, and innovation, DEWA aims to be a leading sustainable innovative global utility.
